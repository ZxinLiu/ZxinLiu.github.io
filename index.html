Zixin Liu

I am a machine learning engineer at Peng Cheng Laboratory. Previously, I received M.Eng. degree from Shandong University advised by Prof. Xuemeng Song & Prof. Liqiang Nie and B.S. degree from Xi'an University of Posts & Telecommunications.

My research interest focuses on Multimodal Learning, Egocentric VideoQA, Computer Vision, and Knowledge Distillation. I'm also very passionate about exploring research areas that are new to me.
